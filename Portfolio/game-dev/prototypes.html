Game Prototypes

Ratsky's Adventures - A working Title

Game Developer & Designer (Assets: Unity Store)

Ratsky Adventures (working title) is my latest project, a historical, environmental narrative stealth and heist game where players control Ratsky, a small but determined rat reclaiming stolen artifacts from the Brattish Museum and returning them to their rightful origins. The game blends stealth, parkour, and historical storytelling, offering an experience that challenges players to navigate complex spaces while uncovering hidden histories.

This project is more than just a game. It's a way for me to refine my coding skills, experiment with immersive design, and push my understanding of game mechanics. As someone who loves environmental storytelling and games like Assassin’s Creed, I’m focusing on crafting a world that feels alive, rich with detail, and deeply engaging.

Cyber Nexus

Level Design, Character Design, 3D Modeling, Coding, Animations, Team Management, Project Manager

Cyber Nexus is a character-swapping puzzle-environmental narrative game set in a high-tech research facility researching a revolutionary radiation cure.

Players take control of Wisp and Aurora, two distinct characters, and must switch between them to overcome environmental challenges, solve intricate puzzles, and navigate the facility while finding out what happened to shut the facility down.

Divers Descent

Game Developer & Designer

DiversDescent is a 2D platformer I created where I designed all the assets, from character animations to enemies, backgrounds, and power-ups.

The game follows a marine biologist who stumbles upon a powerful energy source, which causes irregularities and hybrid mutations in the ocean. These mutations result in dangerous new creatures the diver must face as she embarks on her mission to restore balance.
