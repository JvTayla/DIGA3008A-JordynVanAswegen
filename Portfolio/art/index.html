Art Portfolio

This section showcases my artworks!

I've always had a passion for art and drawing, it's something that’s been with me since I was very toung, maybe 6 years old.

Over the years, I’ve explored both traditional and digital mediums. I’ve been formally taking art since Grade 8, and most of my portfolio comes from my Matric year. I’ve built quite a collection that reflects my growth as a young artist.

My work often pulls inspiration from vibrant, stylized universes like Into the Spider-Verse, Arcane, and Across the Spider-Verse. I love mixing dynamic lighting, storytelling, and stylized characters.

As I grow older, I hope to keep expanding my style, refining my techniques, and using art as a tool to express my thoughts, experiences, and imagination.

I mainly use software such as:

  • Procreate
  • Krita
  • Clip Studio Paint
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Blender

I love combining traditional foundations with digital techniques. Whether it’s character design, portraits, or storytelling illustrations — every piece is a part of my journey.
