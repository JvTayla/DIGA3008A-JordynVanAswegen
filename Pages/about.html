Hi! I’m Jordyn Van Aswegen, a 3rd-year Digital Art student at Wits University, currently working towards my professional degree. I’m currently triple majoring in Interactive Media, Animation, and Game Design. Alongside my studies, I am the proud owner of Tay’s Gourmet Treats, where I craft delicious, custom treats that bring joy to all kinds of celebrations.

I’m based in Johannesburg, Gauteng. When I’m not working on projects or running my business, you’ll find me painting, creating digital artwork, or swimming.
