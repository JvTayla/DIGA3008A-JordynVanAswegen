My Blog posts for the Interactive Media (IM) module are a way for me to track my progress and reflect on what I’ve been learning. They cover how I’m applying the ideas from our readings to my own work and projects. As I go through each week, I break down what I've learned, how it’s shaping my understanding of design and development, and how I’m putting it into practice.

The posts show how I’ve grown over time, from the early weeks to where I am now. I share how I’ve been using what I’ve read in class to improve my skills and how these ideas are influencing my work as I build on my game design, animation, and web development projects.

It’s all about documenting my journey and showing how I’m taking what I’ve learned and turning it into something real and useful for my projects.
