Design Enhancements: Embracing the Comic Book Aesthetic

Jordyn Van Aswegen

Posted: May 23, 2025

Elevating the Comic Book Aesthetic

Hey! I've been on a creative spree, infusing my portfolio with vibrant comic book vibes. Let's dive into the enhancements:

  • Star Pop Animation: Inspired by my love for stars, I developed a "Star Pop" animation featuring a trio of stars that animate with a popping effect, accompanied by randomized images and a delightful popping sound, adding an interactive and whimsical touch.

  • Contact Form Integration Attempt: I endeavored to implement a contact form that would collect data via Google Sheets. Despite following guidelines and scripts, I encountered challenges that prevented successful integration. This experience highlighted areas for growth and troubleshooting.
  • Lightbox for Art Sections: To provide detailed views of my digital and traditional artworks, I incorporated a lightbox feature. This enhancement allows users to view enlarged images with titles and descriptions, offering deeper insight into each piece.

Maintaining Thematic Cohesion

Consistency was paramount. I ensured that all new features aligned with the comic book theme, from visual elements to interactive components. The star animations, in particular, were a personal favorite, adding both aesthetic appeal and a sense of fun.

Enhancing User Experience

To make the site more engaging:

  • Sound Effects: Incorporated audio cues to provide feedback and enhance interactivity.

Technical Refinements

Beyond visual and interactive enhancements, I focused on improving the site's structure and accessibility:

  • Replaced inline image styles with external CSS for cleaner code management.
  • Resolved issues with H1 tags to ensure proper semantic hierarchy.
  • Updated blog posts for better formatting and fixed broken links.
  • Added semantic markup and microformats to enhance SEO and accessibility.
  • Detailed my roles in the Game Development section to provide clearer insights into my contributions.

Conclusion

This comprehensive update not only enriches the visual and interactive aspects of my portfolio but also strengthens its structural foundation. I'm excited for visitors to experience the enhanced design and welcome any feedback!
